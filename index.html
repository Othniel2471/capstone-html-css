Meet our Performers

John Smith

Friday, June 10th at 7:00 PM

John Smith is a singer-songwriter from Nashville, Tennessee known for his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics. He has two albums under his belt and is excited to share his music with a new audience.

Mary Johnson

Friday, June 10th at 7:00 PM

Mary Johnson is a folk singer from the Appalachian Mountains who has developed a unique sound that blends old-time and contemporary folk music. Her lyrics are often inspired by her faith and love of nature.

Rachel Lee

Friday, June 10th at 7:00 PM

Rachel Lee is a pianist and composer from New York City, combining elements of jazz and pop. Her latest album, "City Lights," features original compositions inspired by her experiences living in the city.

Ahmed Khan

Friday, June 10th at 7:00 PM

Ahmed Khan is a sitar player from Mumbai, India, influenced by classical Indian ragas and featuring intricate melodies and rhythms. He has been playing sitar since he was a child.

Carlos Rodriguez

Friday, June 10th at 7:00 PM

Carlos Rodriguez is a flamenco guitarist from Seville, Spain, known for his virtuous playing style and ability to blend traditional flamenco with modern elements. He has won awards for his performances.

Michael Johnson

Friday, June 10th at 7:00 PM

Michael Johnson is a blues guitarist and singer from Memphis, Tennessee who has developed a unique style that blends traditional blues with modern rock influences. His lyrics explore themes of love, loss, and everyday life.
